# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students and individuals aiming to balance their health, study, and wellness routines using an AI-assisted platform.

## Product Purpose
To provide a unified dashboard for tracking health logs, wellness signals, chatting with AI for wellness guidance, and getting medication or lifestyle suggestions.

## Brand Personality
Calm, clean, reliable, empathetic.

## Anti-references
Sterile hospital systems, complex data-heavy analytical dashboards, overwhelming notification-driven apps.

## Design Principles
- Focus on wellbeing and clarity.
- Provide clear and gentle data visualization.
- Empathetic AI interactions without feeling robotic.
- Simplify tracking to minimize cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability.
- Clear visual hierarchy.
- Accommodate diverse user needs without sensory overload.
